# 📘 Cloud Consulting Learnings

This repository documents my practice exercises, quizzes, and frameworks learned while preparing for AWS Cloud Consulting and related professional skills.

---

## 🛠 AWS Tools & Practices
- **AWS Well-Architected Tool** → Evaluate and design secure, resilient, efficient architectures.
- **AWS News Blog** → Stay updated with latest AWS releases and service updates.
- **AWS Solutions Architect – Associate Certification** → Next step for consultants with 3+ years of AWS experience.

---

## 📊 Consulting Scenarios
- **Project Handoff Deliverables** → Goals, deliverables, migration techniques, and outcomes.
- **Networking Strategies** → LinkedIn, DevOps meetups, AWS re:Invent conference.
- **Client Meetings** → Research stakeholders before the first meeting to tailor solutions.

---

## ⏳ Productivity & Prioritization
- **Eisenhower Matrix**:
  - Urgent & Important → Resolve security vulnerabilities.
  - Important but Not Urgent → Develop cost-optimization plans.
  - Urgent but Not Important → Delegate routine tasks.
  - Not Urgent & Not Important → Skip unrelated research.

---

## 🤝 Consultant Responsibilities
- Architect solutions with customers.
- Keep projects on track.
- Upskill teams on AWS services.
- Base solutions on **data**, not instinct or opinions.

---

## 📈 Deliverables & Complexity
- Appropriate deliverable: **Create an EC2 instance and deploy the website**.
- Avoid trivial (logging in, resetting passwords) or overly broad (migrating entire data center) tasks.

---

## 🌐 Soft Skills & Growth
- Be open to feedback from mentors, customers, and peers.
- Improve communication and leadership alongside technical expertise.
- Build a personal brand through consistent networking and knowledge sharing.

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways
- Cloud consulting is a mix of **technical expertise + client advocacy + continuous learning**.
- Success depends on **structured frameworks** (Well-Architected, Eisenhower Matrix) and **professional networking**.
- Certifications like **AWS Solutions Architect – Associate** validate skills and career progression.
